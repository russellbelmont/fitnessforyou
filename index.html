Exercise Info: Squats

Squats are one of the best exercises for strengthening your legs, glutes, and core. Here are some benefits of squats:

  • Builds lower body strength.
  • Increases flexibility and mobility.
  • Helps to improve posture and balance.
  • Boosts metabolism and burns calories.
